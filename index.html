about me

Hello world!


Here is Apple, a CS student of Carnegie Mellon University.

During my university years, I actively explored various engineering fields, including full-stack software engineering, UI/UX design, wearables, robotics, and game development.

I ultimately realized that all these fields are fundamentally connected to software engineering. Therefore, I decided to begin my career as a software engineer to bring more possibilities to the world.

Now, I am dedicated to becoming an excellent Web/iOS engineer. I am passionate about building software that positively impacts people.

In my spare time, I also do painting and writing.
